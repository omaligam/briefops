Methodology & Quality

Why you can trust the numbers

Confidence in a report comes from being able to check it. No black box, no invented figures — here is exactly how every BriefOps report is built and verified.

01

Real data sources

Live data from professional platforms like Semrush, plus public filings, census and trade data. Never AI-guessed numbers.

02

Triangulated & validated

Market size is estimated top-down and bottom-up, then reconciled. No single-source figures — every key number is cross-checked.

03

Every figure cited

In your report, every number carries an inline source you can open and verify yourself. If a claim can't be backed by data, it doesn't ship.

04

Human review

Before delivery, a person checks every claim against its source and flags anything that can't be substantiated. Analysis is written by a human, not auto-generated.
